Run Reset

We started with a configured file of the city.
Feel free to adjust the file.

R = Residential
I = Industrial
C = Comercial
T = Powerline
# = Powerline over a road
. = (Nothing)

Pressing "Run" simulates the growth of the city based on a list of parameters.
Try it out!
